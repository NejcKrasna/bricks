HOW TO PLAY

Use the LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys to move the paddle.

Bounce the picaxe to mine the blocks! Harder blocks take multiple hits to break:

  • Bedrock: 5 hits
  • Obsidian: 4 hits
  • Deepslate: 3 hits
  • Stone: 2 hits
  • Dirt: 1 hit (Drops Power-Ups!)

Catch falling power-ups!

  • Orange Card: Multi-Picaxe
  • White Card: Paddle Expansion
